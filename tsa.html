Overview of the National Technology Student Association

Technology Student Association (TSA) hosts over 60 National Events each year for high school studnts to participate in. Events hosts by the TSA organixation helps high school stents show of their skills in tehcnogloy, mathamtaics, science, and engineering. Students who are part of TSA also gain leadership skills, lifetime friendships, and an unforgettable expeireince. Events range from public speaking and debates to dragster design and structural engineering. Those are some examples of the extensive range of TSA events members can particiepate in.

MOT Charter TSA Officers

MOT Charter High School TSA Chapter

Our Technology Student Organization chapter meets once a week till the competition date where we work on our events and collaborate with each other in a safe, nurturing, and knowledgeable envoriment. Our chapter officers are commited into proper organization and making sure everyone has fun while suceeding in their event. Students are also scheduled to work after school if needed to work on their event. Over the course of the years our chapter has existed, our chapter has been home to state and national winners.
